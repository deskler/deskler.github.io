Face to the technology

We were working on this project for the Ivano-frankivsk's computer school in 2018.

The main problem we were thinking about during this projection was "Artificial Intelligence in bank".

On this web-site you may read about hou people can use artificial intelligence in diffrent ways in bank, such as:
1) Chat-bots
2) Personal virtual assistant
3) Algotrading and robotics
4) Teaching bank assistants
5) Protecting information
6) The efficiency of the offices
7) Bank customer analysis

And of course about artificial intelligence in general.

Finaly there is a prototype of artificial intelligence from wich you can ask simple questions.
